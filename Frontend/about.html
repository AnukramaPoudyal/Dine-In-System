About Us

One Byte Foods is a multicultural restaurant offering homemade tastes from around the globe, right in the heart of Kathmandu. From Nepali, Indian, and Chinese to European and East Asian cuisine, we serve everything. Located amidst the vibrant streets of Kathmandu, our restaurant boasts glazed frontage on two sides, providing views of the bustling city.

Discover One Byte Foods: Where each meal is a story, and every table is an invitation. Join us for unforgettable flavors and effortless reservations.

Our Chef

Meet our talented chef, who brings creativity and expertise to every dish. With a passion for culinary innovation and a dedication to using fresh, seasonal ingredients, our chef crafts delightful creations that will tantalize your taste buds.

Our Philosophy

At One Byte Foods, we believe that great food brings people together. That's why we're committed to creating a welcoming environment where friends and family can gather to enjoy delicious meals and create lasting memories. Our philosophy is simple: good food, good company, good times.

Our Location

You can find us conveniently located in the heart of Kathmandu. Whether you're exploring the area or looking for a cozy spot to dine, we invite you to visit us and experience the flavors of One Byte Foods.
